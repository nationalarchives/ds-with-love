Reputation

Love is a risky business. We hazard our hearts and happiness when falling in love. Marriage or a public relationship can mean hazarding independence, personal wealth and public reputation. Neither of the couples in this case ever married. The barriers that divided them – religion, politics and power – and the risk to their reputations proved too great.

Letter from James Ramsay MacDonald to Lady Margaret Sackville

30 December 1915
